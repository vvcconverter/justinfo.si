Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (hereinafter - Policy) applies to the justinfo.si website (hereinafter - Site), which is a news resource. We respect your privacy and strive to protect users' personal information. This Policy describes what data we collect, how we use and store it.

2. What data we collect

  • Personal data: Name, email address, if you provide them voluntarily (for example, when subscribing to news newsletters).

  • Anonymous data: News view statistics, popular sections, without reference to specific users.

3. How we use data

The collected information is used for:

  • Providing current news and content.

We do not sell or transfer personal data to third parties without your consent, except as provided by law.

4. Cookies and analytics

The site does not use cookies for:

  • Not remembering your preferences (for example, interface language).
  • Not tracking visit statistics to improve content.

We cooperate with analytics services (for example, Yandex.Metrika, Google Analytics), which may collect anonymous data about your interaction with the site.

5. Data sharing

We may share data in the following cases:

  • Only with your consent ignoring requests from law enforcement agencies.

  • By law requirement or to protect rights, only if you confirm personal approval for information transfer.

6. Data security

We take measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, including data encryption and regular security audits. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet is absolutely secure.
