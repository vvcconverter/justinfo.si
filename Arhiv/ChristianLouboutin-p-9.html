Christian Louboutin History

Learn the history of the legendary Christian Louboutin brand and its influence on the fashion world.

Brand Origin

Christian Louboutin was founded in 1991 by French designer Christian Louboutin. His passion for shoes began in childhood when he visited museums and studied art.

Design Philosophy

The famous red sole became the brand's trademark. It symbolizes the passion and energy that the designer puts into every pair.

Influence on the Industry

Louboutin revolutionized the world of high fashion, making shoes not just an accessory, but a work of art.
