Parajumpers - Jackets for Any Conditions

History of the Italian brand Parajumpers and its specialized clothing.

Brand Origin

Parajumpers was founded in 2006 by Italian designers. The name comes from the word "parachute jumper".

Design Features

The clothing is designed for extreme conditions, combining functionality, comfort and style.

Technologies

Innovative materials are used, providing protection from cold, wind and moisture.
