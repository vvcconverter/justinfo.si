Canada Goose - Canadian Brand of Warm Clothing

History of the Canadian brand Canada Goose and its warm clothing.

Brand Foundation

Canada Goose was founded in 1957 in Toronto. Initially, the company produced clothing for Canadian expeditions.

Technologies and Materials

The brand uses goose down and innovative materials to create warm and lightweight clothing.

Popularity and Use

Canada Goose is known for its down jackets and parkas, which are used in extreme conditions around the world.
