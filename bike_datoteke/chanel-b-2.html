Chanel Fashion House History

Learn about the legendary Chanel brand, founded by Coco Chanel, and its influence on the fashion world.

Brand Foundation

Chanel was founded in 1910 by Gabrielle Chanel, known as Coco Chanel. She started with creating hats, but quickly expanded the assortment.

Philosophy and Style

Chanel is known for its elegant and concise style. The icon became the little black dress and Chanel suit.

Influence on Fashion

The brand revolutionized women's fashion, making it more comfortable and practical. Chanel continues to be a symbol of luxury and style.
